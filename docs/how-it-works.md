# How it works

AI Web Co-pilot helps your visitors complete tasks faster by guiding them through your website in real time.

## Overview

Our co-pilot is designed for teams that want to improve conversion and reduce friction on complex flows, such as onboarding, checkout, booking, support, and form completion.

## Setup in 4 steps

### 1) Sign up and describe your website

Create an admin account and tell us:

- **Your site goal** (for example: generate leads, sell products, book appointments)
- **Your target customers** (who they are, what they need, what blocks them)
- **Your domain and key pages** where guidance should appear

This context allows the co-pilot to align guidance with your business outcomes.

### 2) Get your JavaScript snippet

After setup, you receive a lightweight JavaScript snippet generated for your workspace.

The snippet connects your site to your co-pilot configuration and activates the assistant experience.

### 3) Add the snippet to your pages

Include the script in the pages where you want AI assistance.

Once the script is deployed, your site is connected and ready. No heavy integration is required.

### 4) Let customers use the co-pilot

Visitors now see the web co-pilot while they browse and complete tasks.

The assistant provides contextual help, guidance, and next-step suggestions so users can finish actions faster and with less confusion.

## What your customers experience

- Faster task completion
- Fewer drop-offs on multi-step flows
- More confidence while navigating forms and pages
- A smoother, premium digital journey

## What admins can do next

After launch, administrators can continuously refine behavior by updating goals, audience context, and page coverage so the co-pilot keeps improving over time.
